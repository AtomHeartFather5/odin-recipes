French Fries

How to make crispy homemade French Fries

A simple yet tasty recipe to make gourmet level French Fries with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Cut and peeled potatoes
  • Frying oil
  • Various seasonings
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut and peel the potatoes in the classic french fries shape
  2. Add salt, pepper and other preferred seasonings
  3. Heat a pan filled with frying oil
  4. Slowly lower your potatoes for about 10 minutes until crispy
  5. Enjoy!
