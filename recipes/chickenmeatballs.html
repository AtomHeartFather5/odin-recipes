Chicken Meatballs

How to make homemade chicken meatballs

Another simple recipe where we are gonna make baked chicken meatballs perfect to be served with your preferred sauce

    Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • large egg, beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/3 cup breadcrumbs

    Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C
  2. Combine the chicken with breadcrumbs, egg, garlic powder and salt then mix it gently with a fork or hands
  3. Shape the mixture into tiny meatballs
  4. Bake them and enjoy!
